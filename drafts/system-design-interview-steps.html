System Design Interview: Steps

Kunal | Thu 25 March 2021

Step 1: Requirements Clarification

Ask clarifying questions to get a better sense of the system you are building and also which areas the interviewer wants you to focus on.

E.g questions for a Twitter like system -

  • Will users of our service be able to post tweets and follow otherpeople?
  • Should we also design to create and display user‚Äôs timeline?
  • Will tweets contain photos and videos?
  • Are we focusing on backend only or are we developing front-end too?
  • Will users be able to search tweets?
  • Do we need to display hot trending topics?
  • Would there be any push notification for new (or important) tweets?
